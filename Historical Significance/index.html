The Significance Scale

Evaluating ten events from the 1960s using the 5R framework

5R Framework:
  • RRemarkable
  • RRemembered
  • RRevealing
  • RResonant
  • RResulting in Change
How this works: For each event, rate it 1–5 on all five Rs, then write a sentence or two explaining your reasoning. When you submit, you will see a sample analysis with suggested scores and explanations. These are not the only defensible answers — historians genuinely disagree about significance, and your reasoning matters more than matching the suggested scores. Fame is not the same as significance. Some events below are very well known but arguably less significant than their reputation suggests.
